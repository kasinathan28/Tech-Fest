Rules and Regulations

  • Individual competition.
  • Two wiring circuits are to be completed within the time duration.
  • PVC Conduit Wiring must be used.
  • Topic and condition will be provided during the event.
  • Candidates must complete the first circuit and submit the work for inspection. After the inspection, dismantle the circuit and proceed to the next circuit.
  • Tools and necessary wiring materials will be provided
  • Appropriate shoes should be used during the competition for safety purposes.
  • The evaluation is based on the neatness and the time taken to complete the work.

Join us for the celebration of creativity and technology at Carmel Polytechnic College, Punnapra, Alappuzha.
