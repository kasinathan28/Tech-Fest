  • This is an individual competition.
  • Participants are required to complete a specific number of PVC conduit wiring circuits within the given time frame.

  • Candidates must assemble the given circuit within the stipulated time, submit their completed work for inspection, dismantle the wiring, and proceed to assemble the next circuit provided.
