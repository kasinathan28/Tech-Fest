Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must utilize provided tools and machinery following safety protocols.
  • There might be a designated time frame within which participants need to complete the machining task.
  • Participants will be assessed on machining precision, covering dimensions, surface finish, and adherence to specifications for judging.
  • The judges' decision is final in the Lathe Competition, considering participants' precision, adherence to specifications, and surface finish quality.

The Lathe Competition challenges participants to showcase their precision and efficiency in machining on a lathe, with winners determined by both accuracy and speed.
